As a new pilot to wav, we would all appreciate the chance to say hello to you, so head to the New Pilots section of the forum and say hello. You will find that our pilots are a friendly bunch, and always happy to help.

So then, to get started you will find your user account details below, and at this point we would like to remind you that you now have 14 days to complete your first flight and file your first PIREP using the wav acers software. Failure to do so could result in you becoming inactive, and losing your membership, and having gotten yourself this far, we would hate to see that happen.

There is a wealth of information on both the website and the forum about how to get airborne with wav  If you need any assistance from the Management please us via the Contact Ticket system available on the website here {URL_TICKET} or the Forum.

Please note that your forum access is limited for the first 18 days. You can read and search any part of the forum, but you can only post in the New Pilot, Wav cars and Technical Support areas. We'll upgrade your access in a couple of weeks and send you an email to confirm. We encourage you to visit the forum regularly as it's a great place to learn, ask questions and engage with fellow pilots.

Also, if you are a VATSIM member and a route user, you will automatically be invited into the wav Virtual route community within 24 hours, where you will be able to interact with and view the online status of other Wav members. Launch your route tomorrow to see if your invite has arrived!

Finally, we have a Microsoft teams 2 server available which all pilots are welcome to use. It's a good place to meet and chat to fellow pilots. We've created a Microsoft teams account for you already - see your account details above.
